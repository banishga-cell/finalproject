Game Reviews

My honest thoughts on the latest releases and hidden gems. I focus on what makes each game unique and whether it's worth your time and money.

September 2017

⭐ 8

Divinity: Original Sin 2

Larian truly carved out their throne in the CRPG space with DOS2; building on everything that made the first entry great, the combat system that relies on a turn based physics playground is nothing short of a blast.

February 2025

⭐ 7.5

Monster Hunter Wilds

I couldn't have been more excited for a new entry in my favorite monster-slaying series; however, we were met with a confusing mix of fantastically refined hunting mechanics and horrendous technical optimization. Regardless, with patches, Wilds has only gone up in fun.

March 2025

⭐ 8.5

The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion RM

Nothing could've made finals season more difficult to prepare for than a shadow-dropped, top to down remaster of the greatest entry in the Elder Scrolls series. With modernized combat, wildly fleshed out quests, and plenty of demonic Daedra to slay, this is a must play.
